The Best Movies According to Charly

My top 3 movies of all-time

Whiplash

I really love this one because it shows the dedication and commitment it takes to become one of the great ones in anything really. Ignoring the abuse.

Apocalypse Now

One of the most brutal and savage depictions of any war but specially I think it captures really well the essence of Vietnam's Hell, even though I wasn't there.

Amadeus

A great story about vengeance and resentment plus Mozart's greatest compositions, great acting and a big twist that makes things really interesting.
